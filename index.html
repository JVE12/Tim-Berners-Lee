Hello!

Time Berners-Lee is a software engineer, who invented the internet or World Wide Web in 1989 while at CERN, the European Particle Physics Laboratory. He is the directer of the World Wide Web Consortium, a compony that he founded and a Professor in the Computor Science Department at the University of Oxford. In 2001 he became a fellow of the Royal Society, and in 2004 he was knighted.

In 1914 Berners-Lee founded the World Wide Web Consortium(W3C) at the laboratory of Computer Science at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology(MIT) in Boston. It wa smade up of companies that wanted to work together to create Web standards and also improve the web. It was quickly agreed that the technology should be free of royalties and patents. Berners-Lee has served as the director of this W3C since it was founded. The Web was first used for e-commerce in 1997.
